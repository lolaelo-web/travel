Our Strategy Year 1: 0% margin

Why you’ll often see the lowest price on Lolaelo, how we’ll run lean in Year 1, and how this scales without getting greedy.

1) Why are prices so low?

Year 1: we take 0% platform margin. 100% of the payment goes to the partner and payment processing. We absorb operating costs to prove the model.

2) Why do this?

  • Win trust — offer the most cost-effective price with reliable service.
  • Grow both sides — build a base of travelers and a community of partners who keep more.
  • Play the long game — fairness beats junk fees and gimmicks.

We expect a planned loss in Year 1 and will work hard to minimize it.

3) How can we afford it?

  • Fair Margin Formula — small, clear margins × high volume = decent profit over time.
  • Lean ops — automate the boring parts; keep overhead light.
  • Right partners — hotels and vendors who value long-term relationships.

What happens after Year 1?

Year 2: modest fee

Introduce a small, published platform fee. Keep total price competitive and clear.

Scale fairly

As bookings grow, stay disciplined on costs so partners and guests continue to win.

Proudly sustainable

Build a business we’re proud of: fair to partners, fair to customers, healthy for the team.

How we’ll be accountable

  • Publish any platform fee, in plain language, at checkout.
  • No surprise add-ons—ever.
  • Share quarterly notes on improvements, uptime, and support.
