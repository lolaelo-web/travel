Our Strategy Year 1: 0% margin

Radical transparency: why you’ll often see the cheapest price on Lolaelo, how we’ll run at a planned loss in Year 1, and how this becomes sustainable without becoming greedy.

1) Why are prices so low?

In our first year, we charge 0% platform margin. 100% of the booking payment goes to the partner and payment processing. We absorb our operating costs. That’s the bridge we promised to be.

2) Why do this?

  • Build a customer base by offering the most cost-effective price we can—without compromising reliability.
  • Build a partner community of hotels and vendors who share our view: local businesses should maximize profit and keep their fair share.
  • Prove the model: fair pricing + dependable service beats gimmicks and junk fees.

We expect to operate at a loss in Year 1 and will work hard to minimize it.

3) How can we afford it?

Because we’re committed to reasonable margin and a lean operation—not growth at any cost. We keep overhead low, automate where it helps, and choose partners who value long-term relationships over short-term extraction.

What happens after Year 1?

Year 2: modest margin

Introduce a small, published platform fee. Keep total price competitive and transparent.

Scale fairly

As bookings grow, we stay disciplined on costs so partners and guests continue to win.

Proud sustainability

Aim for a healthy, honest business that pays people fairly and avoids the “race to extract.”

How we’ll be accountable

  • We’ll publish any platform fee, in plain language, on the checkout page.
  • We’ll never add surprise add-ons after you start the booking flow.
  • We’ll publish a quarterly note on improvements, uptime, and support metrics.

Be part of it
