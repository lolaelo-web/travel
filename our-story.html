Why Lolaelo exists

Lolaelo began with a simple idea: be a fair bridge between travelers and local partners. Partners should keep more of what they earn. Guests should pay a fair price—without tricks. We choose trust, clarity, and service you can feel.

Our philosophy

  • Fair margin policy. More of each booking stays with our partners and vendors. (Year 1 - 100% of booking cost goes to our partners)
  • Fair price for guests. The most cost-effective price on the market, with the peace of mind that your hosts wins too.
  • Lean & human. We run light, use smart AI, and back it with real people.

How that shows up

Fair-margin mindset

Small, clear margins × healthy volume = sustainable growth for everyone.

Transparent pricing

No hidden add-ons. Partners see what they keep. Guests see what they pay.

Community first

We prioritize locally owned stays and experiences that strengthen their communities.
