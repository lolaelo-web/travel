Why we built Lolaelo

Lolaelo began with a straightforward idea: be an inexpensive bridge between travelers and local partners. Partners should keep more of what they earn, and customers should pay a fair price—without platform games.

Our philosophy

We operate on a simple principle: reasonable margin. We’re here to make a living, not to maximize profit at the expense of partners or guests. When we keep margins modest:

  • Partners retain a larger share of each booking and can invest back into their properties and staff.
  • Customers get a more reasonable price and the good feeling that their hosts are getting their fair share.
  • Lolaelo stays sustainable by running lean and focusing on long-term relationships—not short-term extraction.

How that shows up

Non-greedy by design

We target a small, clear margin. If a fee doesn’t add value, we don’t charge it.

Transparent pricing

No hidden add-ons. Partners see what they keep. Guests see what they pay. Period.

Community first

We prioritize locally owned stays and experiences that strengthen their communities.

What this means for you

Travelers: you’ll see prices that make sense, backed by human support when you need it.

Partners: you keep more of each booking and work with a platform that treats you like a partner, not inventory.

Where we’re heading

In Year 1 we’re prioritizing trust over margin. We’re building a base of customers and a community of hotels and vendors who share our belief that hospitality works best when everyone wins. Read how we’ll do it in Our Strategy.
