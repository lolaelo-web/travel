Why “travel, but kinder”

Lolaelo started as a simple idea: make travel friendlier—for families who book, and for local partners who host. We believe fair policies, clear pricing, and human support create better trips for everyone.

What we stand for

People first

Real humans, not ticket numbers. We prioritize families and the local hosts who welcome them.

Fair & clear

Transparent pricing and policies. No gotchas. Partners know how and when they get paid.

Local matters

We highlight locally owned stays and experiences that give back to the community.

How we got here

2023 — The spark

A family trip exposed how confusing policies and hidden fees can spoil the experience. We wrote the first notes for Lolaelo.

2024 — Prototype

We mocked up a friendlier booking flow and started speaking with independent hosts and operators.

2025 — Partner-first launch in progress

Building with a small circle of founding partners before opening to public waitlist invites.

What’s next

We’re assembling founding partners who believe in hospitality that’s kinder and more transparent. If that’s you, we’d love to connect.
