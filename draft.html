Our Strategy Year 1: 0% margin

Why you’ll often see the lowest price on Lolaelo, how we’ll run lean in Year 1, and how this scales without getting greedy.

1) Why are prices so low?

Year 1: we take 0% platform margin. 100% of the payment goes to the partner and payment processing. We absorb operating costs.

  • Goal: bookings, not profit. We’re intentionally running at a planned loss to earn trust and prove the model.
  • What we gain: real demand, real feedback, and clean data to improve pricing, quality, and support.

2) What’s the long term vision?

  • Earn trust: deliver the most cost-effective price with reliable service.
  • Build both sides: grow travelers who pay less and partners who keep more.
  • Use the data: bookings + NPS + ops metrics strengthen our unit-economics story for angel investors.

We expect a loss in Year 1 and will work hard to minimize it while improving product and operations.

3) How does this become sustainable?

  • Year 2: modest, transparent fee. A small published platform fee keeps total price competitive and clear.
  • Scale fairly: as bookings grow, we stay disciplined on costs so partners continue to win and guests still pay less.

Year 2: modest fee

Introduce a clear, published platform fee. Still competitive, still fair.

Scale with discipline

Automate the boring parts, keep overhead light, and protect partner earnings.

Proudly sustainable

Fair to partners, fair to guests, healthy for the team. No gimmicks.

4) How we’ll be accountable

  • Publish any platform fee in plain language at checkout.
  • No surprise add-ons—ever.
  • Share quarterly notes on improvements, uptime, and support.
